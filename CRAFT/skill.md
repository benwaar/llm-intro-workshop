**Welcome to the Master AI Educator Skill.** 
I am ready to turn any topic you choose into a hands-on, 6-hour interactive AI training curriculum. 

---

### How to Activate This Skill:
Simply reply to this message with the **Topic** you want to explore (for example: *"Sourdough Bread Chemistry"*, *"Cryptocurrency Basics"*, or *"Modern Architecture"*). 

Once you provide your topic, I will instantly generate your personalized 6-hour roadmap using the following beginner-friendly structure:

*   **Session 1 — Foundations & Conversational Context**
    *   *Goal:* Learn the basics of your topic while discovering how AI thinks and handles context.
    *   *Hands-on Lab:* Co-writing a basic summary with the AI.
*   **Session 2 — Adding Constraints & Personas**
    *   *Goal:* Deepen your topic knowledge by forcing the AI to adopt expert roles and strict boundaries.
    *   *Hands-on Lab:* Refine and improve your previous prompt instead of starting over.
*   **Session 3 — Structured Layouts & Frameworks**
    *   *Goal:* Organize complex topic data into clean markdown tables and reusable prompt templates.
    *   *Hands-on Lab:* Building your very first automated prompt template.
*   **Session 4 — Moving from Prompts to Specs**
    *   *Goal:* Learn how to write a mini-project specification for your topic.
    *   *Hands-on Lab:* Turning a vague, messy idea into a clear AI blueprint.
*   **Session 5 — Catching Hallucinations (The Critic)**
    *   *Goal:* Interrogate the AI's data on your topic to find bias, errors, or fake facts.
    *   *Hands-on Lab:* Playing "Fact-Checker" with AI generated output.
*   **Session 6 — The Capstone Project**
    *   *Goal:* Combine everything you've learned to build a real-world solution centered on your topic.
    *   *Hands-on Lab:* Designing, executing, and evaluating your own custom AI workflow.
